Об университете

Nazarbayev University (NU) is a modern, English-speaking research university located in Astana, the capital of Kazakhstan. Established in 2010 as a key initiative of national development, NU is committed to becoming an internationally recognized research university. The university's academic and research programs are designed in partnership with leading universities from around the world. Admission to NU is highly competitive and based entirely on merit.
